SUPER MARIO CHARACTERS
Mario
 ◈ is the protagonist and overall hero of the Mario franchise. Portrayed by an Italian-American plumber, Mario is known for his bravery, determination, and incredible ability to jump and stomp on enemies. He is in possession of a magical pair of overalls, which gives him the ability to defy gravity and navigate through challenging environments

Luigi
 ◈ is Mario's brother and sidekick in the Mario games. He often accompanies Mario on his adventures, using his unique abilities and skills to assist him. Luigi is known for his timid nature and his trademark green-colored clothing, which sets him apart from his older brother. Luigi possesses the ability to jump higher and run faster than Mario, which makes him a valuable asset to the team

Princess Peach
 ◈ is the princess of the Mushroom Kingdom and the main damsel in distress in the Mario games. She is known for her beauty and kind heart. She exhibits a range of abilities throughout the series, such as her ability to throw magic projectiles and float in the air. She has become a beloved character in her own right, known for her bravery and determination
